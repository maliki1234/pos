# Logan POS Pitch Deck Narrative Plan

## Audience

Shop owners, retail managers, wholesalers, and small-business decision makers in East Africa.

## Objective

Introduce Logan POS as the practical, offline-ready sales and retail management system from Logan Tech.

## Narrative Arc

1. Shops lose control when sales, stock, credit, and expenses are scattered.
2. Internet instability makes many POS tools unreliable in daily use.
3. Logan POS brings the core business workflow into one simple system.
4. The owner gets clear reports, including net profit.
5. The business can start small and grow into a managed retail operation.

## Slide List

1. Title - Logan POS by Logan Tech.
2. The shop problem - sales without control.
3. What Logan POS does - one system for daily operations.
4. Core workflows - sell, stock, credit, profit.
5. Offline-first advantage - keep selling when internet drops.
6. Profit and reports - know net profit, not just revenue.
7. Credit and stock control - reduce lost money.
8. Ideal customers - shops, wholesalers, pharmacies, hardware, mini markets.
9. Demo journey - how onboarding works.
10. Next step - book a demo with Logan Tech.

## Visual System

Use Logan Tech logo as the core brand asset. Visual style: dark technology background, electric blue accents, clean cards, and practical retail language.

## Asset Needs

- Logan Tech logo: `marketing/assets/logan-tech-logo.png`
- Editable deck text and shapes.
- Social poster PNG for Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp.

## Editability Plan

All deck copy should be editable PowerPoint text. Logo is placed as an image. Cards, dividers, and CTA areas are native slide shapes.
