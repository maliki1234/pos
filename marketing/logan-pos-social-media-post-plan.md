# Logan POS Social Media Post Plan

Prepared for Logan Tech

## Goal

Launch Logan POS as a practical, offline-ready POS for shops and growing retail businesses in East Africa.

Primary action: **DM Logan Tech or book a demo.**

## Best Platforms

| Platform | Purpose | Recommended Frequency |
|----------|---------|-----------------------|
| WhatsApp Status | Fast local awareness and direct demos | Daily |
| Facebook Page | Reach shop owners, local business groups, and referrals | 4 posts per week |
| Instagram | Visual brand awareness and short feature education | 3 posts per week |
| LinkedIn | Logan Tech credibility, B2B trust, founder/company story | 2 posts per week |

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Share Of Content | Purpose |
|--------|------------------|---------|
| Problem awareness | 30% | Show pain points: manual sales, missing stock, unclear profit |
| Feature education | 35% | Explain offline selling, stock, credit, profit, running costs |
| Trust and brand | 20% | Position Logan Tech as a local tech company for real business needs |
| Conversion | 15% | Ask people to DM, book demo, or start onboarding |

## Posting Rules

- Use simple Swahili-English mix.
- Keep every post focused on one pain point or one feature.
- Do not overpromise with fake numbers or unverified customer results.
- Always include one clear CTA: **DM Logan Tech for a demo.**
- Use the poster asset for launch posts: `marketing/outputs/logan-pos-social-poster.png`.
